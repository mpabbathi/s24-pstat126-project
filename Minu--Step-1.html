Variable Descriptions

The age variable is a numeric variable that lists the age of the patient in years. The sex variable is a binary variable that states the sex of the patient, with 0 representing female and 1 representing male. The chest.pain.type variable is a categorical variable that classifies the type of chest pain the patient is experiencing; 1 represents typical angina, 2 represents atypical angina, 3 represents non-anginal pain, and 4 represents asymptomatic. The resting.bp.s variable is a numeric variable that states the resting blood pressure in mmHg.
